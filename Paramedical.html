Diploma in Medical Laboratory Technology (DMLT) :

Diploma in Medical Laboratory Technology (DMLT) course offered under the State Medical Faculty of West Bengal (SMFWB) is a specialized program designed to train students in the practical and theoretical aspects of medical laboratory technology.

Course Highlights:

Duration :

The duration for DMLT course is typically 2 years and 6 months for internship which includes both theoretical and practical training.

Curriculum :

The curriculum covers a range of subjects essential for DMLT, medical diagnostics and laboratory work. This includes Clinical Pathology, Microbiology, Biochemistry, Hematology, Histopathology and Immunology.

Eligibility Criteria :

Candidates who have completed their Higher Secondary (10+2) examination with Science stream (Physics, Chemistry, Biology) from a recognized board / Council are eligible to apply.

Admission Process :

Admission is usually based on merit in the qualifying examination (10+2), followed by an Entrance Exam conducted by SMFWB based on seat availability.

Course Fees :
  • First Year : Rs. 25,000/-
  • Second Year : Rs. 25,000/-
  • Total Fees : Rs. 50,000/-

Stipend: Students are paid Rs. 2,000.00 per month during the 6-month compulsory internship.

Diploma in Dialysis Technician (DDT) :

Diploma in Dialysis Technician (DDT) course offered under the State Medical Faculty of West Bengal is a specialized program focused on training students in the field of radiological technology and diagnostic imaging.

Duration :

Typically 2 years and 6 months for internship, including both theoretical and practical training.

Curriculum :

Covers Managing Dialysis Procedure and Advances in Dialysis.

Eligibility Criteria :

10+2 Science stream (Physics, Chemistry, Biology) from a recognized board/council.

Admission Process :

Merit-based selection (10+2) followed by an Entrance Exam conducted by the State Medical Faculty of West Bengal.

Course Fees :
  • First Year : Rs. 25,000/-
  • Second Year : Rs. 25,000/-
  • Total Fees : Rs. 50,000/-

Stipend: Students are paid Rs. 2,000.00 per month during the 6-month compulsory internship.
